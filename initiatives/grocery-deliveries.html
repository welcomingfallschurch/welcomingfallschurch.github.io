Neighbors Feeding Neighbors


Building a community of neighbors, one delivery at a time.

Since early 2020, Welcoming Falls Church under the leadership of Shaun and Surekha Cohen has served neighbors in the local immigrant community with access to fresh, nutritious food. What began as an emergency response during the pandemic has become a monthly rhythm of care that connects families across Falls Church.

Our History & Partnership

This initiative began in partnership with CASA, the Mid-Atlantic region’s foremost immigrant advocacy and service organization. CASA provides critical resources, including employment assistance, legal services, and civic engagement opportunities for Latino and immigrant communities. CASA helped us identify the families most in need of support in early 2020.

Why We Show Up

Welcoming Falls Church and Shaun and Surekha have managed and sustained this grocery program since 2020, independently since 2022.

Get Involved: Practical Ways to Help

We rely entirely on the generosity of neighbors like you to keep this program running on the first Saturday of every month.

  • Donate or Drive: We need volunteers each month to provide grocery staples (like rice, beans, pasta, and canned proteins) and to help transport these items to families in the community. We send out a SignUp Genius each month.
  • Join the Team:To receive SignUp Genius emails to contribute to this effort and and get on the volunteer list, please contact our program leads: Contact Shaun and Surekha Cohen

New in 2026-2027: Growing Our Reach

We are entering a season of growth. Our vision is to expand this program by inviting a larger cohort of volunteers and local families to participate. By increasing our volunteer base, we can reach more households and deepen the connections between neighbors throughout Falls Church.

If you’ve been looking for a tangible way to make a long-term impact in your own backyard, now is the perfect time to join us. Please consider becoming one of four to six volunteers or families that help us make this program more sustainable and reach more families. Contact us for more information, and thank you for considering how to help.
