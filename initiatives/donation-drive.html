Donation Drive

Welcoming Falls Church is grateful to the many, many members of the community who donated furniture and household goods to furnish the homes of five refugee families – and because of the generosity of the community to some extended family members of those families, and to some families from our Literacy Center community. We collected couches, dining room tables, side tables, dressers, etc. etc. etc. It was a significant logistical challenge, and we are also grateful to Mark Kawar and Jimmy Jung for coordinating donations at various times for the initiative.

We will inform the community of future needs for furniture and household items.
