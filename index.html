Welcoming Falls Church

Building a community of neighbors. Our goals are hospitality toward immigrants and refugees and opportunity for us all. We believe that by engaging locally we can overcome divisions and reinforce some of our society's greatest strengths. Inspired by a national movement called Welcoming America, we are grassroots, apolitical, and public-private in character. We invite you to learn more and to join us.

Adult and Family Literacy Center

Welcoming Falls Church, in partnership with the Literacy Council of Northern Virginia (LCNV) and Falls Church City Public Schools (FCCPS), is pleased to announce the opening of our Adult & Family Literacy Center!

Registration for adult English classes will take place on January 18, 2022 at 6:30pm in the Oak Street Elementary cafeteria located at 601 S. Oak Street. (Please bring proof of the COVID vaccine.)

Classes will begin in February on Tuesday and Thursdays nights from 6:30-8:30pm. More information about our Spring 2022 pilot program will be coming soon! Until then, contact us at: WFCLiteracy@gmail.com for more information.
